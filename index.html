The Lotus Flower

A lotus flower may not seem like much to think about at first glance, but first impressions can be misleading.
While the plant's symmetry and colors are surely striking, beneath the delicate exterior of a lotus lies a deeper
meaning nearly as old as time.

First and foremost, the lotus flower has a life cycle unlike any other plant. With its roots latched in mud,
it submerges every night into river water and miraculously re-bloom the next morning, sparklingly clean.
As a result, this has led the flower to have associations as the flower with rebirth and spiritual enlightement.

I love the lotus flower because it reminds us that, "beauty is possible even in the darkest of places". So through
all the hardships we may face in life, we'll always conquer.
